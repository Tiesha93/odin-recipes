Cookie And Cream Milkshake

Description

Oreos are a milks favorite cookie, so why not put them in a milkshake? This milkshake is great on a hot summer day, it's a fun and easy recipe to make with your kids!

Ingredients

  • 4 scoops of vanilla ice cream
  • 1/4 cup of milk
  • 4 oreo cookies
  • Whipped cream, for garnish
  • Maraschino cherry, for garnish

Steps

  1. Add ice cream and milk to a blender, blend until smooth
  2. Then add oreos in, blend until crushed to your liking
  3. Pour into a glass and add whipped cream and a cherry
