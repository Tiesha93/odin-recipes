The Best Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe is very easy to make. Lasagna is one of my favorite Italian dishes and that's why this is one of my go to's for a fast and filling feast. It's great for the holiday's, gatherings, or a quick family meal. With the perfect blend of noddles, italian sausage, tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and ricotta cheese melting in your mouth. You simply can't say not to this perfect and simple recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of grounded sweet italian sausage
  • 1 egg
  • 1 box of lasagna noodles
  • 1 jar of tomato sauce
  • 1 container of ricotta cheese
  • 4 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 tablespoon of garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon of parsley
  • 1 tablespoon of italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Bring 2 cups of water to a boil then place noodles in water for 10 mins
  2. Drain noodles and sit them aside
  3. In a 10-inch skillet cook sausage over medium heat until brown
  4. Reduce heat to low and add tomato sauce and let simmer for 10 mins or until thickened
  5. In a small bowl mix ricotta, egg, and seasonings
  6. Preheat oven to 350 F
  7. In a baking dish spread meat and sauce mixture on the bottom.
  8. Top with 4 noodles. Spread ricotta mixture on noodles. Add 1 cup of mozzarella
  9. Add 4 noodles. Top with meat and sauce mixture. Add 1 cup of mozzarella
  10. Add 4 noodles. Spread ricotta mixture and add 1 cup of mozzarella
  11. For your last layer add 4 noodles. Spread meat and sauce mixture. Sprinkle 1 cup of mozzarella
  12. Bake for about 30 mins or until cheese is nicely melted
  13. Let stand for 10 mins before cutting
